# Tutor Portal

The Tutor Portal is the primary interface for individual tutors to manage their tutoring business, students, sessions, and finances.

## Core Features

### Business Management
- [Dashboard](./dashboard.md) - Overview of key metrics, upcoming sessions, recent activity
- [Student Management](./student-management.md) - Student profiles, contact information, session history
- [Importing Students](./importing-students.md) - Bulk import students from CSV/Excel files
- [Family Management](./family-management.md) - Parent/guardian information, family accounts, multi-student households

### Scheduling & Sessions
- [Event Management](./event-management.md) - Session scheduling, recurring events, calendar management

### Educational Resources
- [Lending Library](./lending-library.md) - Track books and materials lent to students
- [Online Resources](./online-resources.md) - Manage and share educational resources with students
- [Homework Submission](./homework-submission.md) - Collect and review student homework assignments

### Financial Management
- [Invoice Management](./invoice-management.md) - Create, send, and track invoices
- [Expenses](./expenses.md) - Track business expenses for tax purposes
- [Other Revenue](./other-revenue.md) - Track additional income streams beyond sessions
- [Mileage Logging](./mileage-logging.md) - Log travel for tax deductions and expense tracking

### Marketing & Content
- [Website](./website.md) - Tutor's public-facing website/profile
- [News & Blog Posts](./news-blog.md) - Content management for tutor's blog and updates

### Analytics
- [Business Reports](./business-reports.md) - Financial reports, session analytics, student progress tracking

---

**Note:** All features should integrate seamlessly with the main platform's scheduling, payment, and communication systems.
